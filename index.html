What I do

Career counseling puts you in the drivers seat. You pay for my services and I work for YOU.

Together we explore the options that most interest you.

Once you determine the field or fields you would like to pursue, we will use current best practices in resume writing, interviewing, and networking to help you lead the most effective job search possible.

I provide individualized career counseling to:

  • New graduates
  • Experienced professionals changing careers
  • Professionals seeking a new position in the same field
  • Homemakers returning to work after a long break
  • Students seeking a new major or direction for college
  • Job seekers suffering from depression and anxiety

We will work together to:

  • Identify goals
  • Create a plan
  • Introduce you to free and low cost job search resources
  • Help create or improve your resume, LinkedIn profile, networking and interview skills
  • Identify efficient, low cost courses to increase or brush-up on skills
